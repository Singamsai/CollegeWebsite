How we teach:

24/7 Portal-After college assistance for students' support. Tailor-made approach to suit the needs of Individual learners.

Structured Instructions-Encourage and teach students to make their own decistions in a classroom setting, thus helping learners to acquire skill based learning.

Classroom Discussions-Questions are discussed in detail by the teacher-mentors in the class, where a student identifies and comprehends intricacies of various kinds of questions.

Guided Practice-Enables learners to analyse and apply the concepts taught.

Independent Learning-An assignment is given to learners in the discussed concepts. The student is expected to work an in-depth understanding and reasoning ability.

Remediation-All necessary and possible remedial steps will be taken-wherever and whenever necessary
